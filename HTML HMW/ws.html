Wind Speed

Wind Speed


Cities between 60 an 80 latitudes have the highest windspeeds followed by cities between the latitudes of -40 and -20. Cities near the equator tend to have less wind speed. Outside of these three findings, there is much more variability in terms of wind speed based on latitiude.
